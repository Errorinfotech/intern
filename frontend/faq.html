Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Error Infotech services and internship programs

What internship programs do you offer?

We offer comprehensive paid internship programs across 12+ domains including AI/ML, Full Stack Development, Graphic Design, Content Creation, Digital Marketing, Performance Marketing, HR Management, and more. Each program is designed with 60-80 structured operations for industry-relevant skills.

How long are the internship programs?

Our programs range from 1.5 months (Basic Package) to 6 months (Enterprise Package). The Pro Package offers a 3-month intensive experience. Each program includes practical training, mentorship sessions, and real-world project experience.

Are the internships paid?

Yes, all our internships are paid programs. Students receive stipends based on their performance and the package they choose. The Enterprise Package offers the highest compensation along with job opportunities for top performers.

What qualifications are required?

Students from any educational background can apply. We welcome freshers, graduates, and professionals looking to switch careers. The only requirement is enthusiasm to learn and grow in your chosen domain.

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes, upon successful completion of the internship, you'll receive an official certificate from Error Infotech. Pro and Enterprise packages also include recommendation letters and internship completion certificates.

What is the application process?

Simply fill out our application form with your details and preferred program. Our team will contact you within 24 hours to discuss your goals and match you with the right program. We also conduct a brief assessment to understand your current skill level.

Do you provide job placements?

While we don't guarantee placements, our Enterprise Package includes job opportunities for top performers. We have partnerships with various companies and regularly share job openings with our successful interns. Our programs are designed to make you industry-ready.

Can I work remotely?

Yes, all our internship programs are available in remote format. We provide virtual access to tools, resources, and mentorship. Regular online meetings and progress reviews ensure you get the best learning experience regardless of location.

What support is provided during the internship?

We provide dedicated mentorship, weekly progress reviews, access to learning resources, and 24/7 support for technical queries. Each intern gets a personal counselor to guide them throughout their journey.

How do I get started?

Fill out our application form on the homepage or contact us directly. Our team will reach out to schedule a brief discussion about your goals and help you choose the right program. Once enrolled, you'll receive access credentials and program details within 24 hours.
